Continuum is a simple widget that shows how much of your waking day has passed.

Set your wake-up and bedtime, and Continuum displays a progress bar showing where you are in your day. Add it to your Home Screen or Lock Screen for a quick glance at your daily progress.

Features:
- Customizable start and end times for your day
- Home Screen widget
- Lock Screen widget
- Clean, minimal design

Perfect for staying mindful of how you're spending your time.
